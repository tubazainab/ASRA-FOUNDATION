About Asra Foundation

ASRA FOUNDATION was founded in 2009 and registered in the Register of Non-Government Organizations, at Nagpur Court House in 2014. His highness late Mujeeb Ashraf Sahab proved the backbone of the foundation in all the services that Asra Foundation is providing.
The Foundation is renowned in the city and one of the best reliable names in the city, the hard work of the NGO makes it a more popular and trusted name amongst the people.

Our Mission

Asra Foundation works for the social development and integration of underprivileged individuals, groups, and communities in India.

Our Vision

Our work aims to break the vicious cycle of poverty and social seclusion and to restore hope for a better future. We believe that every person has the right to access resources and opportunities to live and develop with dignity and to become an active and contributing member of our society.
