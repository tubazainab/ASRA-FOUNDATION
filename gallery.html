About Us

ASRA Foundation was founded in 2009 and registered in the Register of Non- Government Organizations, at Nagpur Court House in 2014

His highness late Mujeeb Ashraf Sahab proved the backbone of the foundation in all the services that Asra Foundation is providing. The Foundation is renowned in the city and one of the best reliable names in the city, the hard work of the NGO makes it a more popular and trusted name amongst the people.
